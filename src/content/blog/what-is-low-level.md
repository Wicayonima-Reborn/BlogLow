---
title: "Apa Itu Low-Level Programming?"
description: "Kenapa low-level ribet tapi bikin ngerti komputer beneran"
pubDate: 2025-01-01
---

Low-level programming itu soal **kontrol**, bukan gaya.

Kalau high-level menyembunyikan detail,
low-level memaksa kamu ngerti:

- Memory
- CPU
- Binary
- Hardware behavior

Ribet? Iya.  
Worth it? Banget.
